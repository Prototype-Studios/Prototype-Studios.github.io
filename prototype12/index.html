The Story

With no memory of what happened before you fell asleep, you realize you aren’t yourself. Instead, you’re a robot with an arm that grants the power of teleportation, escape is now your number one priority!

Features

• Move: W, A, S, D
• Jump: "Space"
• Crouch: "Left Ctrl"
• Blink: "Q" (on surface)
• Object Pick Up & Down: "E"
• Object Throw: Left Click
• Press ALT + F4 to exit the game!
